The Vision

What is Alpha City?

Alpha City is a next-generation blockchain project focused on scalability, security, and community-driven governance. Our mission is to provide the infrastructure for a truly decentralized digital economy.

Tokenomics

The ALPHA Token

The $ALPHA token powers the entire Alpha City ecosystem, from governance to network fees and staking rewards.

Total Supply

A fixed, non-inflationary total supply of 1,000,000,000 $ALPHA ensures long-term value and scarcity.

Fair Distribution

50% Community Treasury, 20% Staking Rewards, 15% Core Team, 10% Ecosystem Fund, 5% Public Sale.

Token Utility

Use $ALPHA for governance votes, staking to secure the network, and as the primary currency in our dApp ecosystem.
